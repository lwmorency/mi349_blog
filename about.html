I’m an Information Science student at MSU who likes building clean, simple web pages and writing about tech, sports, and everyday systems that make life easier. I value practical design, clear typography, and code that’s easy to maintain. When I’m not studying, you’ll find me lifting, gaming, or tinkering with new tools and workflows.

On this site, I publish short essays and experiments. My goal is to share ideas you can try today—small changes that stack up over time. If something helps, feel free to reach out; I like hearing what people build from the same starting point.

Quick Facts

  • Tools I use: HTML/CSS, Git/GitHub, VS Code, basic Python
  • Values: accessibility, readability, and honest metrics
